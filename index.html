The Genesis of IntelGuard

IntelGuard was born out of a singular, unbreakable mission. Founded in June 2025 by our founder, Boaz, the organization was established as a direct response to the pervasive and devastating reality of child exploitation.

After witnessing the scale of abuse from predatory individuals toward the most vulnerable members of our society, Boaz made it his life's goal to turn the tide. He assembled an elite team of professionals to form the cornerstone of this non-profit.
