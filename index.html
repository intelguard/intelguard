About Us

IntelGuard is an organization dedicated to the investigation of crimes against children and the unyielding advocacy for victim safety.

We operate quietly but effectively, utilizing digital analysis and open-source information to identify threats. Our primary objective is to assist relevant authorities in neutralizing these threats and ensuring that perpetrators face justice, while prioritizing the privacy and recovery of those affected.

Our mission is to dismantle exploitation networks and provide a silent shield for those who cannot protect themselves. We believe in action, accountability, and the absolute right to safety for every child.
