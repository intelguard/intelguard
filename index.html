Our Company

What IntelGuard is, and isn't.

IntelGuard is an open-source intelligence outfit built to do one job well: find what's hiding online and get it in front of the people who can act on it. We field a high volume of intake reports every day, running the same kind of triage and case-building work seen in cyber units at police departments — without ever standing in for one.

Our analysts never access or store exploitation material itself. Investigations are built entirely from metadata, infrastructure, network, and open-source signals — enough to identify a host, a domain, or a network, and enough to hand law enforcement a case they can act on immediately.

We currently operate as IntelGuard LLC (registration in progress). We're not a law enforcement agency, and we don't claim law enforcement authority. Every case we build is forwarded to the appropriate agency for their jurisdiction to take from there.

What we do

OSINT-based identification of platforms and infrastructure hosting child exploitation material or facilitating related fraud, built into referral-ready case files.

What we don't do

We don't access, download, or store exploitation material, make arrests, or act as a substitute for law enforcement at any stage of a case.

Where cases go

Completed case files are forwarded to the law enforcement body with jurisdiction — national, regional, or international — for further action.

What We Stand For

Our values.

CHILD-FIRST

Safety before speed

Every action is weighed against what actually keeps a child safer — not what closes a case fastest.

EVIDENCE-BOUND

Facts, not assumptions

We only refer what we can substantiate. Weak leads stay open, not padded into a case file.

IN OUR LANE

Investigate, don't intervene

We build cases. Law enforcement makes arrests. We're careful never to blur that line.

ACCOUNTABLE

Answerable to someone

We're building the formal oversight our work deserves, not asking for trust without it.
