The IntelGuard Mission

Founded in June 2025 by Boaz Acosta, IntelGuard operates as a direct-action intelligence bridge. Our mission is absolute: to dismantle exploitation, extortion, and abuse networks across the globe.

We serve as an elite bridge between digital intelligence and physical justice, coordinating multi-jurisdictional efforts to ensure accountability.
